Income Prediction on the Census Population dataset

Project description:

This project involves data cleaning, data wrangling, feature correlation, model training and model evaluation. The project goal was do understand what factors were significant to determine salary income. The approach for this was a binary classification. The goal of this is to Predict whether income exceeds $50K/yr based on census data and what census features can lead to higher income
